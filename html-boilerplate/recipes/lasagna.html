Lasagna Creation

Lasagna is a dish consisting of long thick noodles which are layered between cheese, sauce and sometimes meat.

  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Groumd beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic
  1. First cook the pasta according to the directions on the box.
  2. While you are waiting, heat the oven to 350 farhenheit.
  3. Next once the noodles are al dente, you can grab a 12x12 baking pan glass or metal
  4. Start a base layer of noodles at the bottom of the pan.
  5. Now in a sauce pan, bring the tomato sauce to a light simmer.
  6. Once simmering add the mushrooms, ground beef, garlic and salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook for about 10 minutes while stirring every few minutes.
  8. Once done, add ricotta to the top of the layer of pasta spreading like peanut butter.
  9. Then spread the sauce as well.
  10. Repeat these steps until there is no pasta left.
  11. Once this is done, you can put the pan in the oven and cook for 30 minutes.
  12. Then take out of oven and cool for 5 minutes and serve.
